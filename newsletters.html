NEWSLETTERS

The CAOSA newsletters are a source of information about the activities of the Association and its members, and also provide historical information about ordnance and the conduct of firing. CAOSA member Gerry de Vries is the Editor of the newsletters and welcomes information about any smooth-bore cannon-related activities. Please send to him at noongun@mweb.co.za
